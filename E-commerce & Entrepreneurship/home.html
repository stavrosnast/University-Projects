CITY CENTER MIRACLE

The brand-new Gilgamesh Hotel lies in the heart of Athens, a perfect base to explore the city on foot, since it offers easy access to the best sightseeing areas. Step out of our 8-room hotel, into the unique buzz of Ermou Street, immerse yourself in the shops, bars and restaurants or escape from the bustle, visiting the archaeological sites and Museums. Situated at the Triangle: Syntagma Square, the Acropolis and Monastiraki, just 300 meters from the Hellenic Parliament and the Syntagma Metro Station. Within walking distance from all the Archaeological Sites and the Plaka.

Gilgamesh Hotel is hosted in a modern building of unique architecture, offering circular rooms with modern design and the only vertical garden in Ermou street! The rooms feature all modern comforts and amenities, along with high tech services and fast internet.

Whether traveling solo, with your significant other or your entire family, our hotel associates will greet you with a smile (and your name!), guide you and provide you with recommendations on making your visit as pleasant as possible. We also pride ourselves on the health and safety protocols and cleanliness of our rooms and common areas, which you will experience first hand during your visit.

A miracle at the very heart of Athens!

We built the hotel as part of the local community and house at the rooftop the famous local wine bar restaurant Manoukas. Start your day enjoying breakfast at the roof top with 360 views of Athens, including the Acropolis, Parliament, and the hill of Lycabettus. Enjoy signature cocktails and a vast selection of wines to accompany every carefully designed plate on the menu. Mingle with the locals who seek a culinary experience at the rooftop of Athens Utopia.

Gilgamesh Hotel is the place to be!

A Smart hotel at the very heart of Athens, combining simplicity with elegance, style with functionality. Enjoy a memorable stay at the Gilgamesh Hotel!

About Us

Hotel Gilgamesh, 5 stars – Accommodation in Athens

Hotel Gilgamesh offers 8 spacious and comfortable modern rooms spread across 2 floors, all accessible by lift. The hotel is situated in a newly renovated building in the residential district of Ermou in Attica. The hotel is an ideal choice for those who are looking for affordable accommodation near in the immediate center of the city.
